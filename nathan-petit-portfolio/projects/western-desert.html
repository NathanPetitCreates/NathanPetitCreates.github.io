Western-Style Desert

Organic Modeling / Environment

This 3D environment captures the raw beauty and solitude of a desert at sunset, blending stylized modeling with a cinematic atmosphere. The scene features a towering wooden windmill, a massive low-poly buffalo skull partially buried in the sand, stylized rocks and dry bushes scattered across the terrain, and several cacti with subtle emissive details to suggest resilience in a harsh environment.

The centerpiece is a twisted, scorpion-shaped dead tree, emerging from a small hill, symbolizing survival and transformation. The lighting is carefully designed with a smooth sunset gradient from deep ochre to dark brown, enveloping the scene in a warm, fading light.
