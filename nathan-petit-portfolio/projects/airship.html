Airship 3D Animation

VFX / Compositing

In this project, I created a detailed concept art piece featuring a retro-futuristic car suspended in a decayed urban environment.The idea was to blend the rugged, worn-out look of 1970s muscle cars with advanced anti-gravity propulsion technology.The vehicle, marked by time and harsh conditions, floats using glowing blue thrusters that replace its traditional wheels.

To enhance the sense of levitation, I incorporated dynamic glows, light smoke effects, and subtle spark particles beneath the car.The background, intentionally foggy and weathered, evokes a post-apocalyptic or abandoned world.A strong depth of field effect naturally draws the viewerâ€™s attention to the main subject, creating an immersive cinematic atmosphere.
