Haussmannian House

Hard Surface / Architecture

This project showcases the 3D modeling of a traditional Parisian-style building and its surrounding environment. Inspired by the architectural elegance of Haussmann-era facades, the scene highlights detailed structural elements such as wooden shutters, ornate window frames, and a decorative rooftop dormer. A climbing plant spreads organically along the side of the building, adding a touch of life and authenticity.

The environment includes a small fenced garden with lush trees, classic benches, wrought iron fencing, and vintage street lamps, all carefully crafted to evoke a peaceful urban atmosphere. Rendered in neutral tones, the clay render emphasizes modeling precision, proportions, and lighting without the distraction of textures or colors.
