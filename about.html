I'm Nathan Petit

A French 3D artist and VFX student specialized in compositing. Passionate about digital creation, I build detailed environments and realistic effects, blending technical precision with artistic direction. My journey into the world of digital arts started at a young age, and since then, I've developed a strong foundation in VFX compositing and 3D modeling.

A creative mind focused on storytelling through visuals.

Whether I'm modeling a Haussmann-style building or compositing shots for a short film, I focus on creating clean, immersive visuals that serve the story. I enjoy designing from scratch, exploring lighting and atmosphere, and pushing the limits of what's possible in post-production.

"Every idea deserves the right tools. I use the ones that fit the vision."
